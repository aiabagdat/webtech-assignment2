Our Recipes

Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • Biscuits
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Fresh strawberries

Steps:

  1. Prepare the biscuit base.
  2. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and strawberries.
  3. Chill for 4 hours before serving.

Macarons

Ingredients:

  • Egg whites
  • Sugar
  • Almond flour
  • Food coloring

Steps:

  1. Whisk egg whites with sugar until stiff.
  2. Add almond flour and food coloring.
  3. Bake small rounds for 15 minutes.

Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients:

  • Cocoa powder
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Red food coloring

Steps:

  1. Mix cocoa, flour, and red coloring.
  2. Add butter, sugar, and eggs.
  3. Bake for 40 minutes, then frost with cream cheese.

Chocolate Delight

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate sponge
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Bake rich chocolate sponge layers.
  2. Prepare creamy chocolate frosting.
  3. Assemble and decorate with cocoa powder.

Strawberry Dream

Ingredients:

  • Vanilla sponge
  • Whipped cream
  • Fresh strawberries

Steps:

  1. Bake a light vanilla sponge base.
  2. Add whipped cream and fresh strawberries.
  3. Chill before serving for the best taste.

Vanilla Cake Bitezly

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cream

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  2. Bake until golden brown.
  3. Frost with whipped cream and serve.
